Use cases

Built for the verticals
where truth is expensive.

Three verticals where real-world ground truth determines real-world outcomes.

Healthcare
Drug availability & formulary verification
No database tracks real-time drug availability. Proxy dispatches healthcare-verified workers to pharmacies and returns structured, confidence-scored inventory data.
  • Is Leqembi 200mg in stock within 5mi of SLC?
  • Does CVS accept Medicare Part B for this drug?
  • Which infusion centers have capacity this week?
Prediction Markets
Physical-world verification for market resolution
Prediction markets need physical verification to resolve correctly. Proxy gives agents a trusted, provenance-tracked ground truth layer for real-world outcomes.
  • Verify construction milestone at this address
  • Confirm public event attendance at venue X
  • Check product launch availability at retail
Retail & Inventory
Real-time shelf availability and pricing
Scraping can't tell you what's actually on the shelf. Proxy sends observers to specific locations and returns structured inventory truth with timestamps.
  • Is Product X in stock at Target on Main St?
  • What is the shelf price vs. advertised price?
  • Verify promotional display compliance
